Frequently Asked

Answers to Your Questions

What are the differences between packages?

+

Starter is perfect for those starting out: 1-page landing + 1 social + 8 posts/month. Professional adds a multi-page website and 2 social channels with 12 posts. Business includes professional photography and 3 social channels with blog and video. Enterprise is fully custom with all channels, video, ads and advanced reporting.

Can I change packages later?

+

Yes, completely. Many of our clients start with Starter and grow to Professional or Business as their business develops. Changes are applied at the next billing cycle.

Does "first month free" really mean free?

+

Yes, the first month of social management is completely free after initial setup. You only pay the one-time fee (setup, design, launch). After the first month, billing starts regularly.

What's included in the "one-time setup"?

+

Website design, development, social profile configuration, Google Analytics setup, basic SEO, SSL, automatic backups, speed testing and mobile optimization. Everything you need to launch online.

Can I see results before committing long-term?

+

Absolutely. We offer a free 30-minute consultation and the first month of management is included in the price. If you're not satisfied after the first month, we can discuss changes or resolve the contract.

What are the contract terms?

+

Packages require a minimum of 3-6 months commitment depending on the plan. This ensures we have time to implement strategies and see concrete results. After the initial period, you can decide to continue month-to-month.

How do ad campaigns work in Enterprise?

+

In the Enterprise package, we set up your advertising campaigns on Meta (Facebook/Instagram) and Google. The advertising budget (the money you actually spend on ads) is separate from our fees and managed directly with the providers.
