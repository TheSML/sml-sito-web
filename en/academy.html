Frequently Asked

Everything You Need to Know

How does the certification work?

+

All courses come with professional international certification. Upon completion, you will receive a digital certificate (and paper, if you wish) attesting to course completion and acquired skills. It is recognized in the job market and you can add it to your LinkedIn.

What's the difference between in-person and online courses?

+

In-person courses (Social Media Mastery, AI for Marketing) are intensive, interactive and ideal if you want to immerse yourself completely. On-demand online courses (Neuromarketing Essentials) let you learn at your own pace, with lifetime access to all materials. Choose based on your learning style.

Can I take the courses if I have no prior experience?

+

Yes, absolutely. Our courses are designed for people at all levels. Social Media Mastery and AI for Marketing start from basics and progress to advanced techniques. Neuromarketing Essentials is recommended if you have basic marketing knowledge.

Which course should I choose if I'm a small business?

+

We recommend starting with Social Media Mastery to learn how to strategically manage your social channels and measure results. Then add Neuromarketing Essentials to understand how your customers think. Finally, leverage AI with the third course to automate and scale. Or choose personalized corporate training.

Do you offer discounts for groups or companies?

+

Yes. For groups of 5 or more people we offer exclusive discounts. Companies can request customized courses with negotiated pricing. Contact us directly to discuss your specific needs and best training options.
